Rankings

Here is a list of all the Ranks available in Luronia Cloud and where they stand in the hierarchy.

Founder
The highest rank of the FC. This is for the one who is running the Free Company.
Master Fighter/Crafter/Gatherer
These ranks are reserved for those who have advanced from apprentice status. Those with this rank are leaders in their respective category, and those who also help run the free company. Feel free to ask Master rank members for help!
Apprentice Fighter/Crafter/Gatherer
These ranks are the standard ranks in Luronia Cloud. Once you have selected your main "rank", you can work towards achieving Master Rank. Ranks can be switched at any time if you would prefer to focus on a different job.
Recruit
All new members will get this rank. You can rank up to an apprentice rank once you are part of the Free Company for 7 days, and you are part of the Luro.io Discord.
