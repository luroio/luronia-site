Hi, we are Luronia Cloud!

A Free Company on Midgardesormr looking to build a great community!

Created by gamers

Luronia Cloud is a Free Company created by the founder of Luro.io. The Free Company started it's journey on July 3rd, 2020, with the goal of creating a great community for helping each other progress in the game and enjoy our time playing it.

Our Mission

Community

We want to build a community of other FFXIV players that are willing to help each other, and just genuinly have fun together.

Progression

We want to help each other progress in the game, and complete content that they want to complete.

Assistance

Need help with new content? Need an item crafted? Feel free to ask in the FC chat! We will do our best to help you!
