These are the rules that all Luronia Cloud members must follow, or they may face expulsion from the Free Company.

General Rules and Guidlines.

Rules and Guidelines are currently in the process of being written. However, as a rule of thumb, Luronia Cloud is a community. As we are a community, you are expected to respect each other. We DO NOT tolerate any sort of racism, sexism, trash-talking, discrimination, hate-speech and anything else in simularity with these. If you are found to have participated in any of these actions, you will face consequences. Respect each other, and have fun!
